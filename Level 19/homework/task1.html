T-34 Tank Information

History

The T-34 was a Soviet medium tank that was produced from 1940 to 1958. It was one of the most effective and influential tank designs of World War II.

Specifications

  • Weight: 26.5 tons
  • Armor: 45 mm to 90 mm
  • Speed: 53 km/h
  • Crew: 4

Impact

The T-34 had a significant impact on tank design and warfare strategy. Its combination of armor, firepower, and mobility made it a formidable opponent. The T-34's design influenced the development of many other tanks during and after World War II.

Variants

Throughout its service, the T-34 was produced in several variants:

  • T-34/76: The original model with a 76.2 mm gun.
  • T-34/85: An upgraded version with an 85 mm gun and improved turret.
  • T-34/76 mod. 1943: Featured enhanced armor and improved features.
